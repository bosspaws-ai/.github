## What we're building

**[BossPaws](https://bosspaws.ai)** is a knowledge-augmented veterinary AI platform engineered exclusively for dogs and cats, with intelligence that extends across health, training, behavior, nutrition, grooming and bonding. It is built around twelve proprietary engineering systems that turn general-purpose language models into a specialist pet intelligence: the **BossPaws AI Engine** (a 2,055-line multi-model orchestrator), the **Dynamic Prompt System** (seven intelligence layers assembled per response), the **BossRadar Context Engine** (priority-scored real-time health injection on a 320–670 token budget, with allergies pinned first for safety), the **Memory Architecture** (eight-category long-term pet memory with scheduled extraction, fuzzy-match contradiction handling, and Haiku-powered profile curation three times weekly), **Video Analysis**, **Multimodal Document Intelligence**, **Weekly AI Insights**, **Weight Intelligence**, the **PawAlert Intelligence Engine**, **FileDesk**, the **PetLife Dashboard**, and **FlexPilot Points &amp; Badges**.

Four capabilities sit at the heart of the platform. BossPaws analyzes thirty-second pet videos through a dual-model pipeline — [Google Gemini](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api) Flash produces an exhaustive veterinary observation report (gait, breathing rate and effort, coat and skin, alertness, pain indicators, posture, training responses), then [Anthropic Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) Sonnet generates a clinical-grade assessment grounded in the pet's full health history and active medications. It orchestrates three distinct models with priority-scored real-time health context injected before every response. It curates long-term per-pet memory into a living profile that evolves with every conversation. And it stores, indexes and re-analyzes vet documents, lab panels and prescriptions in full pet context.

The platform runs on [Anthropic Claude](https://www.anthropic.com/claude) Sonnet, Anthropic Claude Haiku and [Google Gemini](https://ai.google.dev/gemini-api) Flash against a curated veterinary knowledge base of 319 verified breed profiles (225 dog, 94 cat), 225 veterinary conditions across 17 categories, 136 medications and 68 anatomical reference points — every data point sourced from publicly available [AVMA](https://www.avma.org), [AAHA](https://www.aaha.org/for-veterinary-professionals/aaha-guidelines/), [CVMA](https://www.canadianveterinarians.net/) and [WSAVA](https://wsava.org/global-guidelines/) clinical guidelines.

Engineering spans [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org), [Next.js](https://nextjs.org) (App Router), [React](https://react.dev), [Supabase](https://supabase.com) with [PostgreSQL](https://www.postgresql.org) and row-level security, Supabase Auth (magic link and OAuth), [Vercel](https://vercel.com) edge functions and CRON, [Capacitor](https://capacitorjs.com) native bridges for iOS and Android, [CloudConvert](https://cloudconvert.com) (Poppler) PDF vision pipelines, [Recharts](https://recharts.org) data visualization, and [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com). Available on iOS, Android and Web. Built and operated by FlexPilot Inc. in Toronto, Canada.

- **BossPaws AI Engine** — 2,055-line multi-model orchestrator
- **Dynamic Prompt System** — seven intelligence layers assembled per response
- **BossRadar Context Engine** — priority-scored real-time health injection, 320–670 token budget, allergies pinned first for safety
- **Memory Architecture** — eight-category long-term pet memory with scheduled extraction, fuzzy-match contradiction handling, and Haiku-powered profile curation three times weekly
- **Video Analysis** — thirty-second pet video pipeline: Gemini Flash observation report, Claude Sonnet clinical-grade assessment
- **Multimodal Document Intelligence** — vet documents, lab panels and prescriptions stored, indexed and re-analyzed in full pet context
- **Weekly AI Insights**
- **Weight Intelligence**
- **PawAlert Intelligence Engine**
- **FileDesk**
- **PetLife Dashboard**
- **FlexPilot Points &amp; Badges**
